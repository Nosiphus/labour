Monday, November 13, 2017 - Making Fun of Roy Moore, Dicky Goodman style - Sean Crain

We are here at a press conference held by Roy Moore, the Republican nominee for the Senate seat who is under sexual misconduct allegations. Sir, what do you plead?

Nothing to be guilty of. (Guilty - Barbra Streisand and Barry Gibb)

But sir, there have been multiple accusations against you, what do you say to that?

Jive talking, telling me lies (Jive Talkin’ - Bee Gees)

Can you at least explain why they’re claiming this?

I can’t tell you why. (I Can’t Tell You Why - Eagles)

We are now speaking with a woman who claims to have been assaulted by Roy Moore. Ma’am, they say Moore is going to beat these charges. What do you say to that?

Say it isn’t so! (Say It Isn’t So - Hall & Oates)

It’s been two weeks and it has now been confirmed that the events detailed by the women did indeed occur. How were you able to evade charges for so long?

I get by with a little help from my friends. (With A Little Help From My Friends - The Beatles)

What did you think whenever you saw them?

Well, she was just seventeen. Do you know what I mean? And the way she looked was way beyond compare! (I Saw Her Standing There - The Beatles)

What did you tell her?

And when I touch you I feel happy inside. (I Want To Hold Your Hand - The Beatles)

What did she say to you?

I can’t go for that, no, no, no can do (I Can’t Go For That - Hall & Oates)

And what did she say when you continued to harass her?

Help! I need somebody! (Help! - The Beatles)

When did this happen?

It was twenty years ago today. (Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band - The Beatles)
